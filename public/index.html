KidStreet

Let KidStreet guide you: honest scores for how child-friendly London actually is, and what's right for your family. Open data, open methodology.

Methodology: Safety 30% (police.uk crime density) · Education 20% (Ofsted outcomes) · Green space 20% (OpenStreetMap parks & playgrounds) · Transport 15% (TfL + rail access) · Family fit 10% (census) · Planning 5% (approved child facilities) · Composite 0–100, weights renormalised where a ward is missing a metric — no data is never counted as zero. All sources open. Disagree with the inputs? Good, they're published.
