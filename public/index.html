KidStreet

Let KidStreet guide you: honest scores for how child-friendly London actually is, and what's right for your family. Open data, open methodology.

Methodology: Safety 30% (police.uk crime density, inverted) · Education 20% · Transport 15% · Green access 12% · Family fit 10% · Play provision 8% (OS Open Greenspace playable space per child aged 0–15, scored against the London Plan Policy S4 benchmark of 10 m² per child) · Planning 5% · Composite 0–100, missing dimensions excluded and weights renormalised, never counted as zero. Play measures space within each ward, not walking-distance access (that's v2); City of London wards have no published child population, so play is shown as no data there. All sources open. Disagree with the inputs? Good, they're published.
